Placements at Savar Technologies

At Savar Technologies, we are dedicated to not just providing comprehensive training in software development but also ensuring our students launch successful careers in the tech industry. Our robust placement assistance program is designed to connect talented individuals with leading companies in the field.

Our IT On-Job Support mission is to up skill the beginners in IT with the ability to solve their Real-time complex project requirements. We at Savar Technologies, assist and render job support services in IT Courses.

If you are one of those, struggling to survive in the job due to lack of technical abilities or those got a new project and stressed about the work assigned then write to us. We put forward our best skilled IT consultant, to guide you in achieving the best result in your work and furnish your deliverables.

What Set us Apart

Industry Connections

We have established partnerships and strong connections with a vast network of companies across various sectors of the software industry, enabling us to offer diverse placement opportunities.

Tailored Support

Our placement assistance is tailored to individual needs. From resume building and interview preparation to networking events, we provide personalized guidance.

Career Counselling

Our experienced career counsellors provide valuable insights, guidance, and advice to help students make informed decisions about their career paths.

Placement Process

Preparation

We assist students in preparing their resumes, enhancing their interview skills through workshops, mock interviews, and one-on-one coaching sessions.

Networking

We organize networking events, career fairs, and industry talks, allowing students to connect with potential employers and industry professionals.

Job Opportunities

We continuously update students about job openings, internships, and entry-level positions available in reputed companies, ensuring they are aware of relevant opportunities.

Support and Follow-Up

We provide ongoing support and follow-up during the application and interview process, helping students navigate through offers and negotiations.
